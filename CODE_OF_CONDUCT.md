# Code Of Conduct

Open Model Lab aims to keep contribution discussions focused, accurate, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but make technical claims reviewable.
- Keep feedback tied to the code, content, tests, docs, or product behavior.
- Respect user privacy, contributor privacy, and confidential security reports.
- Follow the license, content, brand, and security boundaries in this repository.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private contact details, secrets, customer data, account data, or vulnerability details without permission.
- Spam, trolling, or repeated off-topic disruption.
- Misrepresenting a fork, service, or product as official Open Model Lab.
- Pressuring maintainers to change billing, licensing, security, or brand policy outside the documented decision process.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the project owner or maintainers through:

- email: `feedback@openmodellab.com`
- fallback: the `/contact` page on the live site

Include the relevant links, screenshots, dates, and context when safe to do so. Do not include secrets or private user data.

## Enforcement

The project owner or maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, block users, or take other reasonable moderation steps. There is no separate committee or formal governance body at this stage.
