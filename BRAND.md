# Open Model Lab Brand Policy

Open Model Lab names, logos, icons, marks, domain names, and visual identity assets are all rights reserved. Open-source code and content licenses do not grant brand, trademark, logo, or endorsement rights.

This policy covers:

- the name "Open Model Lab"
- Open Model Lab logos, marks, app icons, favicons, and visual identity assets
- assets under `public/branding/**` and `logo/**`
- confusingly similar names, marks, domains, and product presentation
- official screenshots or product presentation assets when used in a way that implies endorsement

## Allowed Nominative Use

You may use the Open Model Lab name in accurate, non-confusing references such as:

- "built from Open Model Lab"
- "fork of Open Model Lab"
- "compatible with Open Model Lab"
- "uses Open Model Lab source code"
- attribution required by the code or content licenses

Keep this use factual. Do not make a fork, service, course, or product look official unless the owner has granted permission.

## Uses That Need Permission

Do not use Open Model Lab brand assets or confusingly similar marks without permission to:

- name a fork, hosted service, product, course, company, or package as "Open Model Lab"
- imply official endorsement, sponsorship, partnership, or maintenance
- register or use confusingly similar domains, social handles, app names, or package names
- use the Open Model Lab logo or icon as the product mark for a fork or service
- sell services, support, hosting, courses, or content as "official Open Model Lab"
- present screenshots, marketing pages, or app-store listings in a way that suggests a fork is the official site

## Fork Naming

Forks should use their own name and clearly state their relationship to Open Model Lab. A reasonable pattern is:

> Example Learning Lab, a fork of Open Model Lab

Avoid names that make users think the fork is the official Open Model Lab project or deployment.

## Relationship To Licenses

The code license in `LICENSE` and the educational content license in `CONTENT_LICENSE.md` do not grant rights to Open Model Lab names, logos, marks, domains, or brand presentation. Brand permission must be granted separately.
